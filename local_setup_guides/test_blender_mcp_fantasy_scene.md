For a fantasy scene, it's often better to give Blender MCP a **sequence of tasks** rather than one giant prompt. Build the world in layers.

### 1. Terrain & Layout

```text
Create a fantasy environment base:
- A grassy clearing about 50m wide
- Rolling hills around the edges
- A dirt path leading through the scene
- Several large rock formations
- Leave space for a house near the center
```

### 2. Hero House

```text
Create a medieval wizard cottage:
- Stone foundation
- Timber-framed walls
- Steep roof
- Small chimney
- Wooden door
- 4 windows
- Slightly stylized fantasy look
```

### 3. Trees & Vegetation

```text
Populate the scene with vegetation:
- 15 large oak trees
- 20 smaller trees
- Bushes around rocks
- Patches of tall grass
- Wild flowers near the path
- Avoid intersecting the house
```

### 4. Wizard Area

```text
Create a wizard's workspace beside the house:
- Wooden table
- Spell books
- Glowing crystal
- Potion bottles
- Magic circle on the ground
- Small lanterns
```

### 5. Orc Camp

```text
Create an orc camp 25 meters from the house:
- 3 tents
- Campfire
- Weapon rack
- Crates and barrels
- Rough wooden fortifications
```

### 6. Orc Characters

```text
Create several low-poly fantasy orcs:
- One leader near the campfire
- Four warriors
- Simple armor
- Weapons such as axes and spears
- Aggressive poses
```

### 7. Wizard Character

```text
Create a fantasy wizard:
- Long robe
- Staff with glowing crystal
- Standing near the magic circle
- Looking toward the orc camp
```

### 8. Storytelling Props

```text
Add environmental storytelling:
- Broken cart on the road
- Scattered supplies
- Ancient standing stones
- Ruined statue partially covered by vegetation
- Footprints leading toward the orc camp
```

### 9. Lighting

```text
Create cinematic fantasy lighting:
- Golden sunset
- Warm sunlight
- Blue ambient shadows
- Light rays through trees
- Glowing magical effects around the wizard
- Firelight from the orc camp
```

### 10. Final Polish

```text
Improve composition:
- House on the left third
- Orc camp on the right third
- Wizard in foreground
- Mountains in background
- Clear visual path for the viewer's eye
```

### One-shot "art director" prompt

After the basics exist:

```text
Review the entire scene as a fantasy environment artist. Improve composition, scale, lighting, materials, vegetation distribution, storytelling, and visual interest. Make it look like a frame from a modern fantasy RPG cinematic.
```

That workflow usually produces much better results than asking Blender MCP to "make a fantasy scene with a wizard and orcs" all at once.
