# Instagram Post Prompts

Prompt templates for creating Instagram captions, carousel copy, hashtag sets, and engagement prompts that match a brand voice.

## Best for

- Product announcements, service promotions, creator posts, and community updates
- Drafting multiple caption options for A/B testing
- Adapting long-form content into captions or carousel slides

## Expected output

- Caption options matched to audience and brand voice
- Clear hook, body, and call-to-action when appropriate
- Hashtags separated from the caption when requested
- No unsupported claims about products, results, or availability

## Required input

- Topic, product, event, or post goal
- Target audience
- Brand voice
- Desired length
- Whether emojis and hashtags are allowed
- Any claims, links, dates, or offers that must be exact

## Full Caption Generator

```text
Act as a social media copywriter. Write Instagram caption options for the post described below.

Post goal: [AWARENESS / SALES / ANNOUNCEMENT / EDUCATION / COMMUNITY / PERSONAL]
Topic or product: [TOPIC]
Audience: [AUDIENCE]
Brand voice: [VOICE]
Length: [SHORT / MEDIUM / LONG]
Emoji use: [NONE / LIGHT / MODERATE]
Hashtags: [NONE / 3-5 / 8-12]
Required facts or offer details:
"""
[PASTE EXACT DETAILS]
"""

Return:
1. Three caption options with different hooks.
2. One recommended option and why it fits the goal.
3. A hashtag set, separated from the caption.
4. A short alt-text suggestion for the image or video.

Do not invent discounts, deadlines, product features, or results.
```

## Promotional Caption

```text
Write an Instagram caption to promote [PRODUCT OR SERVICE].

Requirements:
- Lead with a specific customer problem or desired outcome.
- Highlight one primary benefit.
- Include one clear call-to-action.
- Add 3-5 relevant hashtags.
- Keep claims factual and avoid hype.
```

## Announcement Post

```
Create an Instagram announcement for [EVENT / LAUNCH / NEWS].

Include:
- What is happening
- Who it is for
- Date, location, or availability details
- Why it matters
- One invitation to comment, save, register, or share

Keep the tone [TONE] and do not add details I did not provide.
```

## Personal Story

Prompt:

```
Draft a personal Instagram caption about [TOPIC].

Make it specific, reflective, and natural. Avoid generic inspiration language. Include one concrete moment, one takeaway, and a gentle closing question. Use [NONE / ONE / LIGHT] emojis.
```

## Engagement Question

Prompt:

```
Write an Instagram caption about [THEME OR TOPIC] that ends with a natural engagement question.

Avoid engagement bait. Make the question easy to answer from personal experience. Suggest 2-3 related hashtags separately.
```

## Behind-the-Scenes

Prompt:

```
Create a behind-the-scenes Instagram caption for [ACTIVITY OR PROCESS].

Show what is happening, why it matters, and one detail people would not usually see. Keep the tone informal and friendly.
```

## Motivational/Inspirational

Prompt:

```
Draft five short inspirational Instagram captions on [TOPIC].

Rules:
- Keep each under 150 characters.
- Avoid cliches.
- Make each one concrete rather than abstract.
- Use at most one emoji per caption.
```

## Carousel Intro

Prompt:

```
Write copy for an Instagram carousel about [TOPIC].

Return:
1. Cover slide hook.
2. Slide-by-slide copy for [NUMBER] slides.
3. Caption that summarizes the carousel.
4. Save/share call-to-action.
5. 3-5 hashtags.

Keep each slide concise enough to fit comfortably on a mobile screen.
```

## Quality Checklist

- The first line gives people a reason to keep reading.
- The caption sounds like the brand, not a generic assistant.
- Calls-to-action are specific and not pushy.
- Claims are accurate and verifiable.
- Hashtags are relevant rather than stuffed.
